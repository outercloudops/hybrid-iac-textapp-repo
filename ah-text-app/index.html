The Founding Mirror

Explore key events, figures, and moments in United States history — from the early colonial period through the close of George Washington's presidency — through an interactive experience that runs directly in your browser.

The program asks questions. You answer them. It is straightforward in that respect.

What it asks you to consider after each answer is a separate matter. Most users find the experience educational. A smaller number find it difficult to characterize. Both responses are noted.

The Founding Mirror

Runs in your browser  ·  No download required

Approximately 10–15 minutes  ·  Ten questions

No account required. No data is stored. The program does not remember you.
It does not need to.

This program covers the period from approximately 1607 through 1797. Questions are drawn from documented historical record. Some answers that feel correct will not be. This is not an error. If you believe your understanding of this period is complete, the program is still recommended. Particularly then.
